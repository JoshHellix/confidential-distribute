## Design System: Confidential Distribute

**Source:** UI UX Pro Max skill — query: `enterprise fintech payment distribution SaaS dashboard`

### Pattern
- **Name:** Enterprise Gateway
- **Focus:** Trust signals, clear role paths (Admin / Recipient), conservative accents

### Style
- **Name:** Minimalism & Swiss Style
- **Keywords:** Clean, spacious, functional, white space, high contrast, geometric, grid-based
